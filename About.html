Our Philosophy

What started off as a fun idea for Christmas gifts for friends and family, blossomed into a very important hobby of mine. I now take pleasure and joy in mixing and experimenting with essential oils sourced from pure extracts of plants sold at my local Farmers Market. Not only are my mixtures locally sourced, but they are also organic and attain the upmost quality. My essential oils can be worn for meditations, stress-relief, relaxation, or even as a perfume. Perfect for any season! Plus, 100% of our proceeds go to charities near and dear to my heart!
